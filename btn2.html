Room To Breathe & Be

The Estates are designed to preserve and protect their treasured landscape — each tailored to the unique topography and gifts of the Virginia highlands. Drawing from historic village traditions, The Estates offer both privacy and connection — a community within a community.

Features / Facilities

Ranging in size from two acres to just under five acres, The Estates promise room for exploration, entertaining, and deep connection.
Each of The Estates are sited at overlook locations with spectacular views.
Estates 1 through 12 boast breathtaking long valley views out to the piedmonts.
Estates 13 through 26 maximize awe-inspiring views out to Talbott Reservoir.
Select estates have longer driveways and more even terrain where elements like a tennis court or swimming pool are beautiful possibilities.
